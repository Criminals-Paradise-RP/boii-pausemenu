RULES



⁍ RP before OOC! Players are not allowed to go OOC for any reason unless being talked to by an admin. If you have an issue with how your RP is going, the best way to get a staff member is to create a ticket in the discord #open-a-ticket channel.



⁍ Killing another player for little or no engaged roleplay is strictly forbidden. Deliberately attempting to start a fight with little to no reason is also not allowed.



⁍ When downed in Phase 1 (knocked out/down), speak & remember interactions leading to injury (act hurt). When in Phase 2 (on your back/fully bled out), you are dead. NO talking or interactions!



⁍ No Powergaming! Power-Gaming is the act of using an in-game mechanic, any external information, or a roleplay concept to favor your own character or story, and give yourself an unfair or unrealistic advantage.



⁍ No metagaming! Metagaming is using out-of-character/external information for in-game situations. This includes stream sniping, 3rd party VOIP applications (discord calls, TS for non-LEO), etc.



⁍ No offensive RP! Roleplaying any type of sexual assault such as any non-consensual touching, rape, etc. is not allowed and will result in an minimal 24-hour ban.



⁍ No combat logging! Logging off or forcibly re-spawning to avoid situations is not allowed. IF you have to log off but are in the middle of an RP situation, you will need acknowledgment from all parties involved.



⁍ You should be respectful to other players and administrators.



⁍ Administrator instructions or rulings are final and non-negotiable. If an administrator's ruling seems to have been made in error the dispute should be handled outside the game via discord by creating a ticket.



⁍ You are not permitted to use a modification that grants you an advantage on the server.



⁍ Criminal's Paradise RP has a ZERO tolerance for sexual misconduct of any nature. Rape scenes of any nature are PROHIBITED, even with consent from other players. Sexual harassment of any degree at any time will be handled accordingly. Zero warnings will be given for violating this rule!



⁍ At no time are you allowed to exploit bugs in-game for personal gain. You are required to report a bug if you find one.



⁍ You are also not allowed to interact between the two characters ie. transferring money or any other assets between your characters or using a third-party "friend" to transfer items between your characters. If this is discovered those items will be deleted from your inventory and you will be banned immediately.



⁍ When robbing another player, you are only able to take what they currently have in their inventory. Players can not rob police officers that are downed or dead (phase 1 or phase 2). You are still allowed to rob police, but while they are alive. Like holding them at gunpoint. When robbing a police officer you CANNOT take their tuner chip. That item is federally protected.



⁍ Knowingly exploiting the script and/or the GTA V engine in order to gain an advantage is not allowed. This includes abusing the physics of GTA V in order to do unrealistic things such as ramping cars off cliffs and continuing to flee.



⁍ Failing to roleplay is strictly forbidden. CPRP is a more realistic roleplay server, you are expected to be roleplaying at all times. If a situation presents itself where rules are broken, roleplay first, report later.



⁍ Do not lie to a staff member about anything related to Criminal's Paradise RP. This includes in-game matters, discord, or any other entity relating to or belonging to CPRP.



⁍ Do not go AFK on the server for any longer than 15 minutes, log out and return when you are ready to play.



⁍ Safe zones are places in the city where the spree of criminal activity is Not allowed. It is FailRP to run away from a crime and enter a safe zone with the intent to escape. If the RP begins outside of the safe zone and is brought into the safe zone you may continue the RP.



⁍ You are not allowed to impersonate emergency personnal (Police & EMS), steal emergency vehicles, or kill or rob EMS/Fire employees (kidnapping/taking hostage is okay).



⁍ If you plan to kidnap EMS there MUST be 1 more EMS on duty and 3 Police officers, if you plan to kidnap or steal from a Civilian there DOES NOT need to be any police officers on duty. And if you want to kidnap or steal from a COP there must be 3 police officers on duty.



⁍ Robber MAY take a friend hostage, but the hostage CANNOT: benefit from the scene (e.i. getting a payout) or help in the scene in anyways (e.i. cannot help fight police or pick up the robbers).



⁍ No more than 4 robbers on the scene of a robbery/crime. Law enforcement will begin conducting the +2 Rule. 4 robbers = 6 officers, if Air 1 is utilized that will be considered 1 officer which will result in 5 officers on the scene in patrol vehicles. (Only exception is the Pacific Standard Bank Heist. In this instance, you will be allowed a max of 6 with cops remaining at +2 with 8 and gang on gang scenes have NO LIMIT)



⁍ No cop baiting. Do not purposefully try and get police attention on you by doing things you would not do with an Officer near you.



⁍ When EMS is on duty, do not respawn without permission. When you are revived, you are in medical custody until the medic clears you.



⁍ If you feel you have been mistreated in a police situation, do not police the police. Use the proper channels (ie. speak to a supervisor, fill out a complaint form for that specific agency). Do not contact an admin unless the officer is clearly breaking a server rule. Take abuse of power as in character and report it as you see fit.



⁍ NO RDM (random deathmatch) or VDM (vehicle deathmatch)! Deadly actions needs to be roleplayed. Killing "on sight" is not allowed and could be seen as a form of RDM.



⁍ If someone pulls a gun or a weapon you MUST listen to them aka Fear RP. If you are already in your car and someone pulls a gun on you, it is acceptable to speed away. (Only hit them with your car if they are in the way of you taking off). At this time you are taking a chance on being shot/shot at. If the cop/suspect informs you to not touch the radio/phone this includes hitting your panic button you should comply or die.



⁍ The administration team may choose to give your character a Death Penalty if you are considered a repeat offender of deadly crimes. A player may decide to perm-death one of their characters. No player will be forced to perm-death a character, except as described above.



⁍ Vigilante/Superhero RP is not allowed. You are not Spider-Man, John Wick, Jason Bourne, Daredevil, etc. Your character is a human and should be roleplayed as such.



⁍ A mandatory minimum of 15 Minutes is required to pass after server restart before any crimes should be committed. This will allow all individuals time to adapt after a server restart and get prepared for any and all crime or interactions taking place within the city. All Crimes should cease 15 Minutes prior to server restart as well, this will help with getting all of our scenes wrapped up.



⁍ While Torture RP is allowed it must be done within reason and you must have a great reason why you are torturing a player. You can not just kidnap someone and torture them.



⁍ Camping illicit spots: Intentionally sitting on high traffic areas that lead to illicit spots (drug areas, job areas, money washing, etc.) is not allowed for the reasons of robbing individuals. Cops are also not allowed to camp drug areas for the sole purpose of making a traffic stop and busting the subject leaving the drug area. Officers are allowed to conduct surveillance to help build a bigger case.



⁍ In order to play on our server, you MUST be able to speak and be understood. You MUST have a working microphone and be able to voice chat in the city.You MUST be on PUSH TO TALK ( Esc > Settings > Voice Chat). The default for push to talk is N. Voice Mods ARE allowed but YOU MUST BE UNDERSTANDABLE.



⁍ Rules are subject to change, be added to, or removed from. Failure to comply with any of these Rules can and will result in a verbal or written warning, suspension, or permanent removal from the community. If there are any questions or you would like clarification on any of these rules, please open a support Ticket.
