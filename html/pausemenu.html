RULES



⁍ Criminal's Paradise RP enforces a strict Zero-Tolerance policy against sexual misconduct. This includes prohibiting any form of rape scenes, even if consensual. Sexual harassment is dealt with promptly, without warnings.



⁍ Engaging in roleplay involving sexual assault or making jokes about it leads to an instant ban. We do not tolerate this behavior in any context, whether verbal or acted out.



⁍ We prohibit racial slurs, sexual harassment, derogatory statements, sexist comments, and any transphobic/homophobic terms. Such behavior is taken seriously and will be addressed accordingly.



⁍ At no time is it allowed or acceptable to “kill off” or delete your character to Avoid Punishment. Also, it is against the rules to transfer your items from one character to another character. By doing this, it's giving “leverage” or “meta inspired” advancement. This offense is punishable by a Permanent BAN.



⁍ VDM/RDM (murder with no cause/RP) is prohibited and will result in immediate consequences up to permanent BAN.



⁍ Simply having no intent/plans to roleplay and or take anything serious within roleplay scenarios. Have intent and reason behind scenes.



⁍ No combat logging! Logging off or forcibly re-spawning to avoid situations is not allowed. IF you have to log off but are in the middle of an RP situation, you will need acknowledgment from all parties involved.



⁍ No power-gaming! Power-gaming involves exploiting in-game mechanics, external information, or roleplay concepts to unfairly benefit your character or story, granting unrealistic advantages.



⁍ You MUST have a working microphone and be able to voice chat in the city.



⁍ Safe Zones are areas on the map where you may not cause violence and or start a priority of any kind. Your scene, if it continues and ends up in a safe zone may continue, however, starting a priority in one will not be allowed.



⁍ After a server restart, a mandatory minimum of 15 minutes must pass before committing any crimes. Additionally, all crimes should halt 15 minutes before a server restarts to ensure smooth scene wrap-up.



⁍ While Torture RP is allowed it must be done within reason and you must have a great reason why you are torturing a player. (You can not just kidnap someone and torture them.)



⁍ Stealing an emergency vehicle from a scene, department or unattended while on business. (The only exception is when actively engaged in a scene with LEO and you use their car as a getaway car) EMS/FIRE is STRICTLY prohibited.



⁍ Ambushing is allowed to an extent. Do not ambush simply because they are an easy target. If you ambush, please have a story behind the attack.



⁍ Driving unreasonably past lore friendly ideologies. Ramping, jumping and a single car flip is okay here and there but a major crash is a major crash, Roleplay is still required.



⁍ Support tickets are to be used to get in direct contact with staff about any issue, concerns, or situations you need assistance with. Do not open tickets to troll staff or waste their time.



⁍ If you have any questions or concerns about the punishment you have received in-game, you MUST open a ticket regarding it.



⁍ Please do not @ someone unless you are in active conversations with them. Pinging the owner for issues that staff handle will not be tolerated (unless actively replying to them or in legitimate conversation.)



⁍ Being disrespectful, ignorant, arrogant, or blatantly toxic towards other members & complaining in chat. THIS CAN and WILL RESULT IN A PERMANENT BAN!



⁍ Disrespect to this community or any of its members will not be tolerated EVER. It is a privilege to play on our server, if you do not like it, leave.



⁍ Private business and/or information regarding Criminals Paradise in any way shape or form is to be discussed in our discord and our discord only. You shall NEVER direct message staff or other members regarding the server.



⁍ If you wish to make a formal complaint and/or give us server feedback feel free to fill out our community feedback or create a ticket.



⁍ DM Advertising your server to our members is simply not allowed. However, we will not stop you from advertising your server via a discord status.



⁍ Discord & FiveMs Terms of Services must be followed at ALL times in our community.



⁍ EMS cannot have physical interaction in crime scenes (blocking, arresting, attacking). They cannot engage in pursuits or crime related scenes, however they can report matching suspects/vehicles and report them to police. They must wait for police authorization to enter the scene and must use proper judgement when police are absent.



⁍ If EMS are on duty, do not respawn without permission. No immediate revenge actions allowed, your injuries must be roleplayed.



⁍ When downed in Phase 1 (knocked out/down), speak & remember interactions leading to injury (act hurt). When in Phase 2 (on your back/fully bled out), you are dead. NO talking or interactions!



⁍ If you feel you have been mistreated in a police situation, DO NOT police the police. Use the proper channels (ie. speak to a supervisor, fill out a complaint form for that specific agency).



⁍ Robber MAY take a friend hostage, but the hostage CANNOT: benefit from the scene (e.i. getting a payout) or help in the scene in anyways (e.i. cannot help fight police or pick up the robbers).



⁍ No more than 4 robbers on the scene of a robbery/crime. Law enforcement will begin conducting the +2 Rule. 4 robbers = 6 officers, if Air 1 is utilized that will be considered 1 officer which will result in 5 officers on the scene in patrol vehicles. (Only exception is the Pacific Standard Bank Heist. In this instance, you will be allowed a max of 6 with cops remaining at +2 with 8 and gang on gang scenes have NO LIMIT)



⁍ Players can not rob police officers that are downed or dead (phase 1 or phase 2). You are still allowed to rob police, but while they are alive. Like holding them at gunpoint.



⁍ YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE TO KNOW AND UNDERSTAND ALL RULES ABOVE. PLEASE BECOME FAMILIAR WITH RULES AS THEY ARE STRICTLY ENFORCED AND UPDATED REGULARLY.
